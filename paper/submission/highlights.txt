Highlights — Solution-Operator Semi-Discretization (SOSD)

- Any Runge-Kutta or collocation step embeds into the multiplication-free DDE framework
- Convergence order raised from 2 up to 2s (Gauss stages) at unchanged O(p) complexity
- Accuracy-sparsity sweet spot located: moderate order with many steps is CPU-optimal
- Shannon-type sampling bound yields an a priori recipe for stage and step counts
- Open-source Julia package SOSD.jl; every figure reproducible by a single script
